Principles · 02 / 21
CarLine

Four things this product is, before it is anything else.

Carline is a piece of furniture in a parent's afternoon. It should feel boring, in the way a well-engineered traffic light is boring. Chick-fil-A simple.

01

One tap, in a moving car.

The primary action is the size of a thumb glance. A parent should be able to tap it without looking, while easing forward in line.

Hit target ≥ 56pt · centered for one-handed reach
02

Calm before urgent.

The UI is quiet — cream, navy, ink. Color is reserved for status. When something changes, the screen tells you, instead of shouting at you.

≤ 2 accent colors visible at a time
03

Glanceable in the sun.

Staff use the tablet outdoors on a folding table. Type is large, contrast is high, status is encoded in both color and shape so it survives squinting.

WCAG AAA on critical surfaces · 18px body min
04

Boring, on purpose.

Predictable patterns over clever ones. Same status chip everywhere. Same primary button. Same way to advance a queue. Trust comes from sameness.

No emoji · no novelty · few illustrations
